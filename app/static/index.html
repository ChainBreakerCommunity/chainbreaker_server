About Us

We are an interdisciplinary team of academics and human trafficking experts from Colombian and USA universities' and ONG's with the support of IBM Traffik Analysis Hub, interested about combating human trafficking using data. Our current research interests are:

  • Knowledge graphs for identifying patterns of human trafficking
  • Semi-supervised learning techniques for labeling sexual advertisements
  • Supervised learning for classifying advertisements

Fighting human trafficking is hard due to the fact that it is required to use several methodologies and techniques from a different knowledge areas, such as machine learning, graph theory, information theory, among others. If you want to contribute to fighting this crime and want to share your knowledge don't hesitate to contact us. We would be more than happy if you want to join to our research team!

How does Chainbreaker work?

ChainBreaker is a data and machine learning service focused on detecting suspicious activities on internet related to human trafficking crime. Getting involed is very simple.

  • 01 Get your account

    Getting your account is the first step to combat human trafficking. Currently, we offer two kinds of subscriptions.

    On the one hand, we offer a free access plan for academic researchers and NGOs interested on do their on research with the data available in ChainBreaker. On the other hand, we offer an advanced plan for private companies and law enforcement institutions, interested not only on using the data, but also on getting access to advanced analytics and machine learning services.

  • 02 Access our data

    Once you get your login credentials, you will be ready to use our service.

    If you are a developer, we offer a python library, namely ChainBreakerAPI that implements several functionalities for accessing and using ChainBreaker data in seconds. Alternative, if you want a more friendly interface, you just need to login into our website by pressing the Login button.

  • 03 Use our machine learning services

    ChainBreaker offers several machine learning services for private companies and law enforcement institutions.

    If you belong to a private company, we offer facial similarity and phone human trafficking scoring risk services so you can include them into your business process. On the other hand, if you belong to a law enforcement institution, you can have access to a list of the most suspicious advertisements and phone numbers per timestamp in order to carry out further investigations.

 

Python API

Human trafficking is a quite complex phenomena and usually is very difficult to research on this crime due to the lack of quality data. That's why we created Chain Breaker API. We want to offer a common data center service where researchers, ONG's, law enforcement organizations and private sector companies can be focused on analyzing information related to human trafficking, instead of spending valuable time on extracting it from several data sources.

Services

Chainbreaker offers different services depending on your needs. If you are an academic researcher or you belong to a NGO focused on combating human trafficking we recommend you to subscribe to our free plan. This plan will give you access to most of the information extracted by ChainBreaker team so you can do you own research. On the other hand, if you work on a private company or in a law enforcement institution, we recommend you to subscribe to our advanced plan, which include services such as facial similarity, phone human trafficking scoring risk and a weekly list of the most suspicious advertisements and phone numbers by timestamp.
